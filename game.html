Games Available

Fifa 19

FIFA 23 is a football simulation video game developed by EA Vancouver as part of Electronic Arts' FIFA series. It is the 26th installment in the FIFA series, and was released on 27 September 2022 for PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, and Microsoft Windows. It is one of the most awaited game by the football lovers.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games that was first released in 2017. It is available on a variety of platforms including PC, Mac, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, and iOS and Android devices. The game is a survival game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing in a post-apocalyptic world. It also has a creative mode where players can build and create their own worlds.

Dota 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena game developed and published by Valve Corporation. It is the sequel to Defense of the Ancients (DotA) mod for Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos. It was first released on July 9th, 2013 for Microsoft Windows, OS X, and Linux. It is free-to-play, with revenue generated by microtransactions. It's also a popular esports game.

The phantom pain

The Phantom Pain is an action-adventure stealth game developed by Kojima Productions and published by Konami. It was released on September 1st, 2015 for multiple platforms, including PS4, Xbox One and Windows. It is set in an open world and features stealth-based gameplay and base-building features. The story follows Big Boss as he seeks revenge on those who destroyed his forces.

Borderlands 3

Borderlands 3 is a first-person shooter game developed by Gearbox Software and published by 2K Games. It was released on September 13, 2019 for multiple platforms including PC, PS4, Xbox One and later on Stadia and Nintendo Switch. The game features cooperative multiplayer, open-world environment and "loot and shoot" gameplay. It received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised its humor, world design, and the variety of weapons and gear available.

Pubg

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game developed by PUBG Corporation. Players are dropped onto an island and must fight to be the last person/team alive while scavenging for weapons and supplies. The game map shrinks over time forcing players to encounter each other. It was first released in 2017 for PC and later on Xbox, PlayStation and mobile devices. It's a popular esports game with tournaments all over the world.
